As the days turned into weeks, I found myself caught in the whirlwind of emotions that had gripped my heart. It was an undeniable truth I couldn't ignore any longer: I was in love. I could almost hear the catchy refrain of a TikTok song playing in my mind, as if the universe itself were validating my feelings.

I then realized, this is love, I know it is.

With each passing moment, Althea occupied my thoughts, not just during the restless nights but throughout the entire day. My phone felt like a lifeline to her world, a gateway to the possibility of connection. Yet, I was held back by the fear of rejection, like an actor hesitating on the brink of a grand performance. I yearned to reach out to her, to send that first message, but my apprehension was a formidable barrier.

All I wanted was to befriend her, to get to know the person behind the captivating aura. I admired her not only for her beauty but for the depth of character she possessed. She had become my muse, my source of inspiration. It was as if her presence had illuminated a path I had never considered, one that led me toward self-improvement and a deeper understanding of what it meant to truly admire and care for someone. In the midst of uncertainty and self-doubt, one thing was crystal clear: my feelings for Althea were genuine, and they were here to stay.
