As the days turned into weeks, my heart found itself entangled in a web of emotions that left me questioning the very nature of what I was experiencing. I couldn't deny the persistent thoughts that occupied my mind, the way I caught myself gazing at her during school, and the longing to share moments alone with her, laughing and enjoying each other's company. Was this love, or was I simply confused by the intensity of my feelings?

The more I pondered, the more convinced I became that what I was feeling was indeed love. It wasn't just a passing infatuation or a fleeting attraction. It was a deep, profound connection that transcended the ordinary. My heart seemed to have made its decision, and the girl who had captured my thoughts day and night was undeniably Althea Cometa.

With each passing moment, I found myself drawn further into the enchanting world of Althea. Her presence had become a constant in my life, and I couldn't imagine a day without thinking about her. The mere mention of her name sent a rush of emotions through me, a mix of excitement and trepidation. The boundaries of friendship and something more had blurred, and I was left wondering if this was the beginning of a love story I had always dreamed of.
